What goes into your weight?

These player weights are determined for standard games only from the beginning of the echo cluster. You may update the records by using the "update data" button. If no new games have finished since the last time it was updated, it will say so - Otherwise, give it a few seconds to call the api.
You only get points for finishing games.
300 points for a 1st place finish.
200 for 2nd.
150 for 3rd
100 4th
75 5th
60 6th
50 7th
40 8th
30 9th
20 10th
10 11th
You get 1 point for every planet you have ever held at the end of a game.
You get 1 point for every turn you have played in a game that you finished.(Even if you joined mid-game, if you finished, you get 1 point for every turn you played)
You get 0.5 points for every ship you had at the finish of the game.
You get 0.25 points for every starbase you had at the finish.
And you get 0.00005 points for every militaryscore "point" that you had at the finish of a game.
